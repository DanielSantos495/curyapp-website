2. What the service is

SEO Analyzer is a Shopify embedded app that:

  • Reads the on-page SEO fields of your products (meta title, meta description, image alt text, description length, URL handle) and reports which fields are missing, too short or out of recommended range.
  • Surfaces a dashboard summarising your catalog, groups issues by type, and lists your lowest-scoring products.
  • Generates alt-text suggestions for product images that don't have one, derived from the product title and the variant name, and applies them to your Shopify catalog when you trigger the bulk-fix or single-product fix action.
  • Exports the latest audit as a CSV file.

The App is provided as software-as-a-service, embedded inside the Shopify Admin via Shopify's App Bridge.
