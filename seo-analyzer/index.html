Shopify App

Audit on-page SEO. Bulk-fix missing image alt texts.

SEO Analyzer reviews the on-page SEO fields of every product in your Shopify store — meta title, meta description, image alt text, description length and URL handle — and lets you fix missing alt texts across the catalog in one click.

Customer Service

What it checks

  • Missing or out-of-range meta titles (ideal 50–60 characters).
  • Missing or out-of-range meta descriptions (ideal 120–160 characters).
  • Product images without alt text.
  • Product descriptions shorter than 100 characters.
  • URL handles that aren't search-friendly.

What it does NOT do

  • It does not estimate Google ranking or organic traffic.
  • It does not promise SEO improvement — completing field hygiene is necessary but not sufficient.
  • It does not analyze pages, collections, blog posts, HTML structure or Core Web Vitals.
  • It does not use AI in this release.
